Air Quality Prediction Model

Using Machine Learning to forecast air pollution levels.

The Project Story

Air pollution is a major environmental and public health concern. The ability to accurately predict air quality in advance can help authorities issue warnings and citizens take precautionary measures. I developed a data-driven model to predict the Air Quality Index (AQI) using historical data on pollutants and meteorological factors.

The project involved extensive data cleaning, exploratory data analysis to find correlations, and feature engineering to create more predictive inputs. I trained a Decision Tree Regressor model, which proved effective in capturing the non-linear relationships in the data. Dealing with missing data and outliers was a significant challenge that required careful imputation strategies, providing a deep dive into the practical machine learning workflow.

Technology Stack

  • Python
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn
  • Scikit-learn
  • Pandas
